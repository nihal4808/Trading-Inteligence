# Roadmap Source

Paste your uploaded roadmap content here.

Import rules (supported patterns):
- Week headings like: "Week 1 — Market Foundations" or "## Week 1: Market Foundations"
- Day headings like: "Day 1 — ..." or "### Day 1: ..."
- Bullet tasks under a day, optionally prefixed with a category like:
  - "[Psychology] Manage tilt triggers"
  - "Risk Management: Position sizing exercises"

The importer is idempotent: it will only import when the roadmap tables are empty.
